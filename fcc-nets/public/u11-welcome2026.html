Our Vision for U11 This Season

Season 2026 · Cricket With Purpose
"One Team, One Dream"
Dear U11 Families,

We are genuinely excited about the 2026 season and wanted to share our plans with you — so you feel part of what your child is working towards this year.

This season, we plan to be more intentional about how we develop each player. That doesn't mean more pressure — it means more purpose. Every session will have a clear goal, every player will receive personal attention, and we aim to keep you updated on how your child is growing throughout the season.

What Matters Most to Us

Our first priority is that your child loves cricket. A child who falls in love with the game at U11 is the player who still plays at 21.

To help the development of your child's cricketing skills, we assess each player only against their own previous performance — and we celebrate every step forward, however small.

We use a coaching style called implicit coaching — instead of telling players exactly what to do, we ask questions that make them think, experiment and discover for themselves.

There are a few things that genuinely make a difference:

👏

Celebrate effort, not just results

A good catch or a well-played shot deserves to be celebrated — effort and attitude are what we reward.

🤐

Let us do the coaching

Even well-meaning advice from the boundary can confuse. Trust us to guide them on the field.

📅

Consistent attendance

Every session builds on the last. Consistency really helps your child progress.

🏟️

Show up for matches!

Your presence gets the kids going and keeps them highly motivated. It means the world to them!

🙋 Support Parent Program

Each session, we need one volunteer parent present alongside coaches — helping with equipment, keeping energy positive, and supporting safe group management.

This is not a coaching role — it's a helping hand. We will set up a rota so the commitment is shared fairly.
